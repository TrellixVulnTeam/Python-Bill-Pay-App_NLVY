About BillPay:

Online BillPay is a free and secure web base online bill payment platform service that allows customers to pay their monthly bills and receive notification and up-to-date information on their account from the comfort and safety of their homes.

Why online BillPay:

BillPay App was created because of the problems brought about by COVID 19 pandemic. Prior to the pandemic it took approximately 1 -2 hours to pay a bill at any utility company. Today the problem is intensely frustrated, because of the restrictions and the mandatory need to practice physical and social distancing, while standing in long lines. These problems are affecting the timely collections of payment from customers throughout the island.
