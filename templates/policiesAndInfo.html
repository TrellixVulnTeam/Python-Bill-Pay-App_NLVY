Step by Step Instructions for Bill Pay:

  • First Create an Account
  • Type in First Name
  • Type in Last Name
  • Type in Email Address
  • Type in Home Address
  • Type in Phone Number
  • Type in Password
  • Confirm Password

Create a Password:

  • Account should not have less than 6 characters
  • Re-Enter Password

Log in Instruction:

  • Type in your email address
  • Type in your password
  • Click on Remember Me (save your password)
  • Press Login

Bill Payment Instruction Page:

  • Click on the drop-down triangle Icon-top right-hand corner
  • Select which company bill you you paid to view payment details
  • You can also select all to view all company payment details

Next Page:

  • Click on Utility Company, to Select the Company Name you are Paying your bill to
  • Click on Bank Name, to Select your Financial Institution, For example, Grenada Co-Operative Bank
  • Utility Account Number: Add the account number of the Utility Company you are paying your bill to
  • Amount Paying
  • Debit Card is not Required at the moment
  • CVC Number is not Required at the moment
